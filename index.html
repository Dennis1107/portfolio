TSNewsvendor Python Package

2019 Data Science

Master thesis: Data-driven Newsvendor Model

During my Master thesis I developed a data-driven newsvendor model considering substitution effects and censored demand to optimise the production programme of a medium-sized bakery. The original Newsvenor Model implies that there is a lost sale when a stockout occurs. In reality this assumption does not hold as people just tend to buy similar products (substitution effect). My approach took this into account by using Machine Learning and Operations Research methods.
